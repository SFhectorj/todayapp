## Name
***Problem Statement***: Students on average take *6 classes* a semester which means requiring *80* hours a week to study and additionally deal with extra curricular activities. Busy lifestyles often lead to issues with *health, stress* which affects their daily life.

***Solution***: A lifestyle app for students that *personalizes* their daily tasks, keeping in mind their most important obligations (work and extra-curriculars). This app accepts syllabus/assignments and other files to help learn more about the user and to create a personalized calendar detailing what should be completed each day while recommending wellness activities when needed.
